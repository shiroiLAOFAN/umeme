ABOUT U MEMES

U Memes is a website for people who interested in university life experiences and serve as a documentary project to archive interesting moments. Meme itself has strong impact because it can be simply created, downloaded, shared, reproduced, remixed, and reedited. The website encourage viewers to create your own memes based your experience and feel free to screenshot or download the images from this website. Users can also contact me through Contact page and submit the memes that you want to upload to this website. The website highlight how memes reflect university life and diverse cultures, particularly for young audiences.
